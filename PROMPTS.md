# AI Summary Prompt

## Prompt

Generate a professional AI spend optimization summary for a startup team.

Include:
- Overspending observations
- Duplicate tooling concerns
- Optimization opportunities
- Savings potential
- Maintain a professional SaaS consulting tone

Limit:
100-120 words.

## Why This Prompt

The prompt was designed to generate concise audit summaries that sound realistic for startup finance and engineering teams.

The goal was to avoid overly generic AI-generated language while keeping recommendations actionable and readable.

## Fallback Strategy

If the AI request fails:
- show a templated optimization summary
- continue rendering audit results normally
- avoid blocking the user workflow
