# Economics

## Lead Value Estimation

A converted lead could be highly valuable for Credex because startups purchasing AI infrastructure credits often spend thousands of dollars annually.

Assuming:
- average converted customer value = $3,000/year
- Credex margin = 20%
- effective profit per converted customer = $600/year

Even a relatively small number of conversions could justify the acquisition strategy.

## Customer Acquisition Cost

Estimated CAC by channel:

| Channel | Estimated CAC |
|---|---|
| Hacker News | $5-10 |
| Reddit | $10-20 |
| Twitter/X organic | $15 |
| Founder communities | $8 |
| SEO/blog content | $20-30 |

Because the product is naturally shareable, social acquisition could remain relatively inexpensive during early growth.

## Conversion Funnel

Example funnel:

- 10,000 visitors
- 2,500 completed audits
- 500 email captures
- 75 consultation requests
- 15 paying customers

That would represent:
- 25% audit completion rate
- 20% lead conversion rate
- 20% consultation conversion rate
- 20% sales close rate

## Path to $1M ARR

Assuming:
- $3,000 average annual customer value
- 333 paying customers needed for $1M ARR

The product would need:
- consistent organic traffic
- strong social sharing loops
- partnerships with startup communities
- ongoing AI tooling benchmarking

The key growth driver would likely be viral report sharing rather than paid advertising alone.
